Mix together:
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1/2 cup cocoa
- 2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 tsp salt
Cool. Sift together:
- 2 cup flour
- 1 1/2 tsp soda
In another bowl mix:
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
Add the dry ingredients alternately with the milk mixture. 
Mix thoroughly (no need to really beat it). 
Bake at 350 F till done.
Makes 2 layers or a 9 x 13" cake.
